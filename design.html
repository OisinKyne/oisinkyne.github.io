Growing up in a family-run Web Design & Computer Training business has resulted in me learning many of the involved skills. Hopefully my web design ability is apparent through the use of this website. One of the other skills I've come to learn is Graphic Design.

I completed an ICM accredited course in Web and Graphic design at thirteen years of age. I hadn't much use of the skills I learned for a few years though. However, on coming to college in UCD I discovered there was a huge demand for amateur graphic designers for the design of posters for the hundreds of different events the many societes of UCD host.

I personally did a lot of work with the Cumann Gaelach in UCD, a 1,400 member strong society of people interested in the Irish Language. In my second year I was elected the technical supervisor on the comittee. As a result, I designed and still design many posters for the society.
